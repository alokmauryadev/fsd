Accelerating workforce dexterity to maximize digital transformation

Overview

A workplace transformation strategy is becoming increasingly dependent on digital dexterity as a catalyst. The workforce must adjust to the new working methods and pick up the newest digital skills and technologies to accelerate digital transformation and spur large-scale innovation. Our Digital Literacy Buddy (DLB) is a digital adoption platform and a no-code software that can be integrated with any web-based application to increase the digital adoption rate. It provides on-screen, real-time, interactive in-app guidance and automates repetitive workflows. The DLB also gives intelligent insights on user engagement across the underlying applications, friction points and process and goal completion rate, besides providing complete visibility into technology stack adoption across the organization.

DLBs Layered Approach to User Adoption
